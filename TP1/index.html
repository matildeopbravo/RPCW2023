About Hungary Cities Budapest
  1. The capital of Hungary is Budapest
  2. The population of Hungary is 9,627,000
  3. Hungary claims no official religion and guarantees religious freedom. More than one-third of the people are Roman Catholic, most of them living in the western and northern parts of the country.
  4. Hungarians, who know their country as Magyarország, “Land of Magyars,” are unique among the nations of Europe in that they speak a language that is not related to any other major European language.
  5. Hungary was founded back in 895, which means it’s older than countries like France and Germany.
  6. At the end of World War I, Hungary lost 71 percent of its territory as a result of the Treaty of Trianon (1920).
